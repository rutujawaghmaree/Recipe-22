🛒 Ingredients:

  • 3 boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 2 green chilies (chopped)
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 8-10 curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander (chopped)
  • Oil for frying
  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • ½ tsp red chili powder
  • Pinch of baking soda
  • Water to make batter
  • Salt to taste
  • 6 pav buns
  • Green chutney, tamarind chutney, dry garlic chutney

👩‍🍳 Instructions:

  1. Make the Potato Filling: Heat oil, add mustard seeds, curry leaves, green chilies, and turmeric. Add mashed potatoes, salt, coriander, and mix well. Let cool. Shape into balls.
  2. Prepare the Batter: Mix besan, chili powder, salt, and baking soda. Add water to make a thick, smooth batter.
  3. Fry the Vadas: Dip potato balls in the batter and deep-fry until golden brown. Drain on paper towels.
  4. Assemble: Slice pav, spread chutneys, and place vada inside. Press gently.
  5. Serve hot with fried green chilies and chutneys.

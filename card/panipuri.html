🛒 Ingredients:

  • 20 puris (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup boiled and mashed potatoes
  • ½ cup boiled chickpeas
  • ½ tsp chaat masala
  • 1½ cups mint leaves
  • ½ cup coriander leaves
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1½ tbsp tamarind pulp
  • 1 tsp roasted cumin powder
  • 2 cups cold water

👩‍🍳 Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Filling: Mix mashed potatoes, chickpeas, chaat masala, and salt.
  2. Make the Pani: Blend mint, coriander, green chilies, ginger, tamarind pulp, cumin, black salt, and water. Strain and chill.
  3. Assemble: Crack the puris, fill with stuffing, and pour in chilled pani just before serving.

💡 Tips & Variations:

  • Add boondi to the pani for extra texture.
  • Use sprouted moong for a healthier version.
  • Adjust spice level to taste with more/less chili.

🛒 Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 cups spinach (blanched and pureed)
  • 1 onion, 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  • ½ tsp garam masala, ¼ tsp turmeric
  • 2 tbsp cream or milk (optional)
  • Salt and oil

👩‍🍳 Instructions:

  1. Heat oil, add cumin seeds, sauté onions and ginger-garlic paste.
  2. Add tomatoes, turmeric, salt, and garam masala. Cook until soft.
  3. Add spinach puree and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Add paneer cubes and simmer for 3-4 minutes.
  5. Finish with cream or milk. Serve hot.

💡 Tips & Variations:

  • Soak paneer in warm water for soft texture.
  • You can use baby spinach for sweeter flavor.
  • Try adding kasuri methi for aroma.

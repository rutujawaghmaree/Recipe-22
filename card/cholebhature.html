🛒 Ingredients:

For Chole (Spicy Chickpeas):

  • 1 cup dried chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 2 tea bags or 1 tsp tea leaves tied in cloth
  • 1 bay leaf
  • ½ tsp garam masala

    • For Bhature (Fried Bread):

    • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
    • 2 tbsp semolina (sooji)
    • 1 tsp sugar

    👩‍🍳 Instructions:

    Preparing Chole:

    1. Pressure cook soaked chickpeas with tea bags, bay leaf, black cardamom, and salt for 5 whistles. Discard tea bags and set aside.
    2. In a pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds, cinnamon, green cardamoms, and kasuri methi. Sauté until aromatic.
    3. Add chopped onions and ginger-garlic paste. Cook until onions turn golden brown.
    4. Mix in turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, garam masala, and amchur. Sauté for a minute.

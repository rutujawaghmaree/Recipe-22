🛒 Ingredients:

  • 300g cleaned prawns
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Fresh coriander for garnish
  • Salt and oil

👩‍🍳 Instructions:

  1. Heat oil, sauté onions until golden. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook till aromatic.
  2. Add tomato puree, turmeric, chili, and salt. Cook until oil separates.
  3. Add prawns and cook for 5–6 minutes until just done.
  4. Sprinkle garam masala and stir well. Garnish with coriander.
  5. Serve hot with rice or chapati.

💡 Tips & Variations:

  • Do not overcook prawns — they get rubbery.
  • Add coconut milk for a Malvani-style twist.
  • Use mustard oil for extra flavor.

🛒 Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rajma (kidney beans) soaked overnight
  • 3 cups water (for pressure cooking)
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 medium tomatoes (pureed)
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

👩‍🍳 Instructions:

  1. Pressure Cook Rajma: Add soaked rajma and 3 cups water to a pressure cooker. Cook for 15–20 minutes (or 5–6 whistles) until soft.
  2. Prepare the Masala: Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds. Once they splutter, sauté onions until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, cook for a minute. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  4. Spice it Up: Add all the dry spices and mix well.
  5. Combine Rajma: Add cooked rajma (with water) to the masala. Simmer for 15–20 minutes on low heat.
  6. Garnish: Top with fresh coriander and serve hot with steamed rice.

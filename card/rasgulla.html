🛒 Ingredients:

  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice or vinegar
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 4 cups water
  • 2-3 cardamom pods (optional)

👩‍🍳 Instructions:

  1. Boil milk and curdle it using lemon juice. Drain using a muslin cloth to get chenna.
  2. Knead the chenna for 10 minutes until smooth. Make small smooth balls.
  3. Boil sugar and water with cardamom. Drop the balls and cook covered for 15 minutes.
  4. The balls will expand. Turn off heat and let cool in the syrup.
  5. Chill and serve soaked in syrup.

💡 Tips & Variations:

  • Use only fresh milk to get soft chenna.
  • Don’t overcook or the rasgullas will become rubbery.
  • Add rose water or kewra essence to the syrup for aroma.

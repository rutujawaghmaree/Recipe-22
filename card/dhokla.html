🛒 Ingredients:

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1 tsp ENO fruit salt
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp ginger-green chili paste
  • ½ cup yogurt
  • ½ cup water
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp oil, mustard seeds, curry leaves, green chilies (for tempering)

👩‍🍳 Instructions:

  1. Mix besan, turmeric, salt, yogurt, and water to form a smooth batter.
  2. Just before steaming, add ENO and mix quickly.
  3. Steam in a greased plate for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Prepare tempering with mustard seeds, curry leaves, and green chilies. Pour over dhokla.
  5. Cut into pieces and serve warm with chutney.
